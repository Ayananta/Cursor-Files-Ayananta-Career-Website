Preface

If any natural phenomenon of a high degree of complexity is to be broken up into its most fundamental component, what would emerge is something astonishingly simple. This is a truth that is built into our universe. A simple concept, mechanism, or design has the potential to generate something stupendously complex.

Being someone who explains technical concepts to customers and other users and one who's mindful of the maxim that simplicity is the ultimate sophistication, I believe that if what I write cannot be understood by the masses, then I have not understood the concept clearly enough. So, I aim to go right to the bottom of any technology and strive to understand the underlying fundamental concept. Once the fundamentals are clear, explaining any technology or mechanism in a way that everyone understands, no longer remains that big of a challenge.
